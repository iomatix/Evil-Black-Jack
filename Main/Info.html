Rules of the
The Evil's Blackjack

In each turn player rolls two devil's dice and adds the numbers to his total.
Player can roll as many times as his wish.
Once player has scored 13 or more, he has the option to stop rolling or just roll one die one or more times.
If his score goes over 21, then he busts.
The aim is to score 21 or get closest to 21 without going over.
The player's opponent is the Evil.
If no player scores 21 then the player closest to 21 gets 1 point.
If both players have the same score, then no point is given to any player.
The game is played for multiple rounds and the first player to reach to 3 wins the game.
If player losts his soul will belong to the Evil one.
If player won, the Evil would fulfill one winner's wish.
